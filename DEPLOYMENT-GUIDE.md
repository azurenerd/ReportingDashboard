# Executive Dashboard - Deployment & Startup Guide

## Overview

This guide provides step-by-step instructions to build, run, and verify the Executive Project Reporting Dashboard on your local machine.

## Prerequisites

- **Operating System**: Windows, macOS, or Linux
- **.NET SDK**: .NET 8.0 or later
- **Browsers**: Chrome/Chromium or Edge (latest versions)
- **Port Availability**: Port 5000 and 5001 must be available
